# Research Intake

```yaml
research_intake:
  every_n_prs: 5
  deep_review_every_n_prs: 10
  on_new_domain_or_architecture_decision: true
  entries: []
```

Research is controlled input to review and backlog renewal. Do not run broad external research for every ordinary PR.

External ideas must become notes, rejected ideas, or candidate backlog items with verification paths before implementation.
